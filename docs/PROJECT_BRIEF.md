# Project Brief

## Name

Gribo Digital.

## Definition

Gribo is an editorial laboratory for digital systems, cultural infrastructure, and living research.

## What Gribo Is

- Editorial laboratory.
- Digital systems magazine.
- Living research archive.
- Cultural infrastructure space.
- Applied research surface.
- Public home for a future conversational entity called Gribo IA.

Gribo Digital is the public house of Gribo: its voice, first impression, archive, memory, editorial lines, project repository, technical notes, and contact surface.

## What Gribo Is Not

- Not a generic blog.
- Not only a portfolio.
- Not only technical documentation.
- Not a corporate CMS.
- Not a heavy enterprise application.

## Brand Intent

Gribo should have its own voice. People should not choose Gribo only because it has more information, but because they connect with how Gribo speaks, thinks, structures, and visualizes digital systems.

The product should feel editorial, technical, warm, precise, and alive. It should make unfinished systems readable without flattening them into generic content cards.

## Current Product Stage

Stage 1 created the Nuxt foundation: public routes, Gribo Studio placeholders, documentation routes, Nuxt Content collections, visual tokens, and mock content.

Stage 1.1 stabilizes that foundation and aligns the documentation with the broader strategic direction before any larger feature work begins.
