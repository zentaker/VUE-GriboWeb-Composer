The internet is usually described as fast, but speed is only the surface metric.

What changes a life is proximity. A file, a person, a room, a market, a reference, a language, a city: each can become reachable enough to matter.

Proximity is the quiet layer beneath the interface. It is where infrastructure becomes feeling, and where distance stops being only geography.
